About Us

With over 40 years of combined experience in electroplating and manufacturing, we specialize in high-quality earthing and grounding solutions. Our journey began in Goa and expanded to Kerala, serving industries including Siemens, Godrej, and Crompton.

We deliver durable, corrosion-resistant products designed for long-term performance in industrial, commercial, and infrastructure applications.

  • Continuously innovate manufacturing processes
  • Build long-term customer relationships
