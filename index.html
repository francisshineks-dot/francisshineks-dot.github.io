About Us

With 40 years of combined experience in manufacturing and electroplating, we are a trusted manufacturer of earthing and grounding solutions. Our company was founded in Goa, where we built strong expertise in industrial electroplating, serving leading organizations such as Siemens, Godrej, and Crompton, along with antique and vintage vehicle restoration projects that demanded high precision and durability. In 2009, we expanded to Choondal, Kerala. While our operations initially focused on electroplating, we evolved to meet industry needs and today specialize in the manufacturing of high quality earthing products. Supported by our in depth electroplating expertise, we ensure superior corrosion resistance, conductivity, and long service life. Our product range includes Earth Rods, Lightning Arrestors, Earth Chambers, and Terminal Strips, manufactured to meet safety and performance standards for industrial and infrastructure applications. Built on experience, engineering knowledge, and quality driven processes, we deliver reliable earthing solutions designed to last.

Our Products

Lightning Arrester

Efficient lightning protection system.

The Oscar Copper Bonded Lightning Arrester is engineered to provide reliable and efficient lightning protection for buildings and critical infrastructure. It features a air terminal design manufactured with 99.9% pure electrolytic grade copper bonding to ensure excellent conductivity and fast dissipation of lightning currents. The arrester is built with a stainless steel core, nylon insulated base, and uniform copper coating of 250/100 microns (average), offering superior corrosion resistance and long service life. Designed in compliance with IS/IEC 62305 3:2010 and IEC 62561 2:2018, it is ideal for industrial, commercial, and residential lightning protection systems. Product is available in single spike and multi spike.

Earth Rod

High conductivity grounding rod.

Copper Bonded Earth Rod (12 mm, 100 Microns) is engineered for efficient and dependable earthing systems. It features a high-tensile steel core with a uniformly bonded 100-micron copper coating for excellent electrical conductivity and superior corrosion resistance. Designed for long service life and consistent performance, it is suitable for industrial, commercial, residential, and lightning protection applications. Easy to install and low in maintenance, it ensures safe and reliable grounding. The product is available in different lengths: 2,3,4 and 5 Feet

Earthing Compound

Improves grounding performance.

Oscar Earthing Electrical Compound is a high quality, mineral based compound engineered to significantly reduce soil resistivity and enhance the performance of earthing and grounding systems. Designed for consistent results across all seasons, it absorbs and retains moisture effectively, ensuring stable earth resistance even in dry conditions. Non corrosive and environmentally safe, Oscar Earthing Compound protects earth electrodes from corrosion while improving electrical conductivity around the grounding area. With a service life of over 25 years, it is an ideal solution for residential, commercial, industrial, solar, and infrastructure earthing applications. Available in 5 KG and 8 KG bags.

Earth Plate

Large surface grounding solution.

Oscar Engineering Copper Bonded Earth Plates are manufactured from high conductivity copper bonded material to deliver efficient and long lasting grounding performance in varied soil conditions. Designed with a large surface contact area, these plates ensure effective dissipation of fault currents and lightning surges, particularly where earth rods cannot be installed. The copper bonded construction provides excellent corrosion resistance and durability, making them suitable for residential, commercial, industrial, and lightning protection earthing systems. Available in multiple standard sizes for diverse installation requirements. Available sizes 600*600 300*300

Earthing Pipe

Reliable grounding pipe.

Earth Pipe is a reliable and economical solution for effective electrical grounding. Manufactured from high quality galvanized iron, it provides good mechanical strength, corrosion resistance, and efficient dissipation of fault currents into the ground. Suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial earthing systems, it ensures safe and stable grounding performance. Oscar offers three earthing pipe variants in 1 meter and 1.5 meter lengths: GI Earthing Pipe, Compound Filled Earthing Pipe, and Copper Bonded Earthing Pipe, allowing flexibility to suit different soil and application requirements.

Terminal Strip

Safe earthing connections.

The Earth Bench / Terminal Strip is manufactured using a high conductivity copper or copper plated bar with a standard width of 25 mm and a thickness of 4 mm, ensuring excellent current carrying capacity. It is mounted on insulated holders for safe and secure installation and is designed to allow easy connection and disconnection of earth conductors. The terminal strip offers minimal risk of bi metallic corrosion, providing long service life even in demanding environments. Manufactured in accordance with applicable IEC guidelines, it delivers reliable electrical conductivity and robust mechanical strength for long term earthing and equipotential bonding applications. Available in 4 hole, 6 hole, 8 hole, 10 hole and 12 hole configurations to suit different earthing system requirements.

Earth Pit Chamber

Inspection chamber.

Earth Inspection Pit Chamber is manufactured from high density, UV stable polymer material to ensure long term resistance against weathering, soil conditions, aging, and cracking. The square type chamber provides safe enclosure and easy access for inspection, testing, and maintenance of earthing and lightning protection systems. Supplied as a ready made unit with a removable lid, it eliminates the need for on site masonry work and enables quick installation. Designed for dependable performance in residential, commercial, and industrial applications, the chamber complies with IEC 62561 5 standards and is dimensioned approximately 24 × 18 × 21 cm.
